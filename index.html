About Us

At Cabcom Dispatch, we specialise in providing expert call handling and taxi dispatch services for private hire and taxi companies across the world. Our team consists of highly trained and experienced dispatchers and call operators who understand the fast-paced nature of the taxi industry.

Our team is trained on leading UK taxi dispatch software, including iCabbi and Autocab. We pride ourselves on delivering accurate, friendly, and efficient service with every call. Whether it’s taking bookings, managing school runs, or supporting night shifts, our controllers work as an extension of your business.

We know how important reliability is in this industry. That’s why we offer dependable support, tailored to your company’s needs — helping you reduce missed calls, increase bookings, and provide a better experience for your customers.

We’re more than a service — we’re your dispatch partner.
